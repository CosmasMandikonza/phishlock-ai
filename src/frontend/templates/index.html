    About PhishLock AI

    Created for the SANS AI Cybersecurity Hackathon

    PhishLock AI is an advanced open-source phishing detection platform that combines the power of large language models, behavioral analysis, and threat intelligence to detect even the most sophisticated phishing attempts.

    According to the 2024 IBM Cost of a Data Breach Report, phishing is the #2 cause of data breaches, averaging $4.88M per breach. As attackers increasingly use AI to create convincing phishing messages, traditional detection methods are becoming less effective.

    Our solution addresses this challenge by using a multi-layered approach:

    • RAG-Enhanced AI Analysis: Using retrieval-augmented generation to provide the AI with up-to-date phishing knowledge
    • Behavioral Analysis: Detecting manipulation tactics common in phishing
    • Advanced URL Analysis: Identifying suspicious domains and links
    • Brand Impersonation Detection: Recognizing when trusted brands are being imitated

    PhishLock AI is open-source and designed to be easily integrated into existing security infrastructures.

About PhishLock AI

An open source project aimed at making advanced phishing detection accessible to everyone.

PhishLock AI was created to address the growing sophistication of phishing attacks. Traditional security tools often struggle with modern social engineering tactics, while AI-based solutions can be expensive or lack transparency.

Our goal is to provide a free, open-source solution that combines multiple analysis techniques with explainable AI to detect even the most sophisticated phishing attempts.

Key principles of our project:

  • Accessibility: Free and open for everyone to use and modify
  • Transparency: All decisions are explainable and understandable
  • Privacy: Analysis happens locally without storing sensitive data
  • Community-driven: Built with contributions from security experts
